About this Frame + Style Library | Specialty Styles

Highlighted on these last frames is the library of basic styles that this design will include. Fundimentally the different style building blocks that will be available for building pages on the site. This is by no means all of the styles, as specific styles can be created as needed. This is merely a showcase of what can be expected.

A bit more robust than your typical block style, Specialty styles can be classified as styles for carousels, image galleries and or other more interactive styles.
